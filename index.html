About Us

Discover the story behind TSUEE FHOP: Coffee and Hugs.


Founded in 2023 by a group of friends with a shared passion for coffee and a genuine belief in the power of human connection, TSUEE FHOP is not just a café; it's a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the warmth of heartfelt hugs. The concept originated from the idea that a simple cup of coffee has the potential to foster meaningful connections and spread joy.


Meet the Team!

Estrella Rose Ann Navia

Owner

Meet Estrella Rose Ann Navia, the heart and soul behind TSUEE FHOP: Coffee and Hugs. As the founder and owner, Estrella is not just passionate about coffee; she's on a mission to create a haven where every sip of coffee is accompanied by the warmth of genuine human connections. With a background in business and a deep love for crafting delightful coffee experiences, Estrella ensures that each cup served at TSUEE FHOP is infused with love and care.
